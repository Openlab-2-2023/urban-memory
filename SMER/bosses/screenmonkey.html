Folding Screen Monkeys

Folding Screen Monkeys (見る猿、聞く猿、言う猿、, Seeing Monkey, Hearing Monkey, Speaking Monkey,). 4 wise monkeys hidden in Senpou Temple 

you must defeat them to order get a Mortal Blade.
    
    
    Fight is very different than every other in game. Youll met 4 monkeys:
    "See no evil"- Purple robe, good eyes but bad hearing
    "Speak no evil"- Orange robe, when sees you she will start ring and all monkeys flee 
    "Hear no evil"- Green robe, good hearing bad vision
    "Do no evil" - White robe, invisible make foot prints where he stand. It is a play on the oft forgotten "Fourth Monkey," of the original "Three Wise Monkeys," proverb and mythology
