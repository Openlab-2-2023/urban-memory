Corrupted Monk

Corrupted Monk (real name Yao) (破戒僧, Depraved Monk)
The Corrupted Monk can be fought twice throughout the game: once with just one health bar and then again with three depending on the chosen ending.

Fight:

The player must fight the single health bar illusion version in Mibu Village of the Ashina Depths to collect the Shelter Stone in order to proceed through the story.
She is not an Apparition type enemy, so Divine Confetti will not deal extra damage to her however she is an illusion so, Snap Seed, Fistful of Ash and/or Lazulite Axe can be used to briefly stun her; with that in mind, it is possible to perform stunlocks and prevent any counter attack
