Fight:

2 phases each have one Deathblow:
First phase is most about trowing kunai at the player. Second is about illusions.

Phases:

FIRST PHASE ATTACKS:
Kunai Throw: All kunai can be blocked or deflected.
Jump into the Ranfters: She can be shot down by Shuriken Prosthetic, but most likely when youll shot late she will deflect it.
Perilous Attack - Sweep: Jump on her head and deal some Poster damage.
Perilous Attack - Above: Dodge out of way slightly. After this attack is small window to hit Lady Butterfly.

SECOND PHASE ATTACKS:
Illusions: Lady Butterfly snaps with fingers and summon army of Illusions. Use snap seed to instatly kill enemies or you can wait to vanish them to projectile and hide behind a piller.
