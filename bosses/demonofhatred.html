Demon of Hatred

Demon of Hatred (怨嗟の鬼, Demon of Resentment, Ensa no Oni)
Man turned in demon because of his immense wrath and bloodlust burning now stands Ashina Outskirts. For the sake of Ashina and its people, it must be dealt with.

FIRST PHASE ATTACKS:

Explosive Body Slam-Fire spreads to his entire body. Everything will be brighter. He will crouch down to ready the jump.

Hand Drag Charge- Demon will hit with his right arm to the ground.

Fire Whip Slam- His left hand he will use like a whip.

Double Fire Throw- Only occurs at medium range. His left hand will pull back. Fireball Summon- Only occurs at range. Both hands will go up and will start waving. Fire Melee Combo- Occurs in melee range. Winds left hand back.

SECOND PHASE:

Fire Whip Slam Explosion- Similar to the normal Fire Whip Slam, but his body will have more fire and be brighter. The normal Fire Whip Slam may still occur.

THIRD PHASE:

Fire Whip Swing Combo-Danger Indicator and sound will show. His full body will wind up for the attack.

Double Hand Drag Charge-This will usually occur in lieu of the single charge. Danger Indicator and sound will show. His right hand go back and down to the ground.
