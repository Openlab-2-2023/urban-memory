Genichiro Ashina

Genichiro Ashina (葦名弦一郎, Ashina Genichirou) is very first boss youll meet. In first encounter you cannot defeat him.
The man who stole the Wolf's Lord at the beginning of the game, Genichiro Ashina is holding him at the top of a building in Ashina Castle. He wields a large bow along with his two handed katana.

Second time youl meet him in Ashina Tower. After defeating him he will tranform in: Genichiro Way of Tomoe

FIRST PHASE ATTACKS:

Leaping Slam into Thrust Attack - After being attacked, Genichiro will launch himself into the air and execute a sword slam.
Can be sidestepped for a few hits. If parried, Genichiro will follow up with a Thrust attack that can be Mikiri Countered.

Floating Passage - Genichiro executes the Floating Passage Art. After Genichiro perfectly parries Sekiro, he will begin a long string of sword attacks concluding with the Floating Passage.
It is advised to only attempt to block or deflect his attacks while at high posture.

SECOND PHASE ATTACKS:

Sword Dive Thrust Attack - If idle, Genichiro will attempt to interrupt healing by charging Sekiro with a sword dive. This can be Mikiri Countered or deflected.

Electric slash - After the warning, jump in the air. If you are hit by the attack, press attack before you land to deflect electricity back. Does heavy health damage and stuns Genichiro.

Electric slam - Same as Electric slash.

Pro tip: Repeatedly spam Shadowrush. This move will slowly chip away at his HP bar without him counterattacking, allowing you to finish him off or more easily fill up his Posture gauge.
