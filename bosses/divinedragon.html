Divine Dragon

Divine Dragon（桜竜, Sakura Dragon）
A Divine Realm. Place where a Divine Dragon lives. His tears can cure sickness or end Immortal Serverance.

FIRST PHASE ATTACKS:

In first phase youll will not fight divine dragon directly, but the Old Dragons of the Tree (白木の翁たち, White-wooden Old Men) which is a very simple fight. As the enemies move very slowly and do not charge directly at the player.

SECOND PHASE ATTACKS:

In the second phase the player finally faces the Divine Dragon. Attemping to approach it in close range will cause the Divine Dragon to summon a mighty gust that will push the player far away.
In order to damage the Divine Dragon, the player needs to run towards the trees that are charged with electricity and hit Divine Dragon with spear of lightning.
After defeating Divine Dragon youll get a GRACIOUS GIFT OF TEARS.

I very recommend to fight this Boss all alone for best experience.
