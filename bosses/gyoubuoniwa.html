Gyoubu Masataka Oniwa - Gyoubu the Demon

Fun fact: Gyoubu's horse is named Onikage (鬼鹿毛) "Demon-like Bay Horse"

The first Shinobi you'll ancounter. Gyoubu uses long spear named Yari.
He is found in Ashina Outskirts right past the sculptor's Idol.
He is not optional. Must be defeated to advance in the story.

Fight:

- 2 Health bars
- required 2 Deathblows to kill this Boss.

You have two options how to defeat this boss. First is fight him face to face. Second is method called "Gyoubu skip". Gyobu skip is not intended way how to kill him but is interesting way to challange up the game.

PHASES ATTACKS:

First and Second Phase:
Standart Combo (Overhead,Downward,Long Overhead): First easy to deflect, second fast but can still be perfectly blocked, third is over his head and by deflect will cause Gyobu stagger.
Detaching Thrust: Throw a spear with rope. Can be deflected.
Jumping Attacks: 3 jumps with spear pointed at you. Can be dodged or deflected.
Detaching Sweep: When player is far Gyobu starts swing with tip of his spear on rope hitting large circle around him.
